Moovie

User Experience / User Interface

Moovie is a social platform designed to connect cinema enthusiasts through sharing and gameplay. The app allows users to review the films they've watched and discover new titles based on recommendations from their own social circle, effectively eliminating the indecision of movie night.

Furthermore, the platform integrates a gamification component that enables users to challenge their contacts to film trivia quizzes, transforming a passion for the big screen into a dynamic, competitive, and community-driven experience.
